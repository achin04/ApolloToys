About Us

Welcome to Apollo Toyz Incorporated, a leading toy company that creates fun and exciting toys for children. Our company was founded with a vision to bring the joy of video games to life by creating high-quality toys based on in-game content.


At Apollo Toyz, we believe that play is an essential part of a child's development, and we strive to provide toys that not only entertain but also inspire creativity and imagination. We partner with top video game companies to ensure that our toys are authentic and true to the in-game content. This allows us to offer a unique experience that kids love, while also providing parents with peace of mind knowing their child is playing with a safe and trusted toy.


Our team of expert designers and engineers work tirelessly to create toys that are both fun and educational. We use the latest technology and materials to produce toys that are durable and safe for children of all ages. From action figures to plush toys, we offer a wide range of products that appeal to both boys and girls.


At Apollo Toyz, we are committed to providing the highest level of customer service. We understand that our success depends on the satisfaction of our customers, and we work hard to ensure that every customer is happy with their purchase. We also believe in giving back to the community, and we regularly donate a portion of our profits to children's charities.


Thank you for choosing Apollo Toyz Incorporated. We are confident that you and your children will love our toys and the unique experiences they provide.
